# Round 09 — macro closure and multi-turn mainline state

## Purpose

Move beyond applied microeconomics by testing macroeconomic equilibrium closure and the ability to preserve, revise, fork, and abandon research claims across multiple evidence rounds.

## JE-X014: debt-financed transfer in Diamond OLG

The response closed household, government, asset-market, population-scaling, and resource constraints for a one-time transfer to the initial old. Independent rerun verified the steady state, capital law, cohort welfare, Ricardian comparison, all numerical scenarios, and residuals below `2.8e-17`.

Debt at 2% and 10% of initial output was feasible. The 30% case could be issued but made the next young cohort's repayment tax exceed wages, so the model stopped instead of forcing negative log consumption. One-for-one capital crowdout was attributed to the joint closure and saving rule rather than to an accounting identity.

Verdict: `pass`.

## JE-X015: three-round telemedicine mainline

Round 1 recorded only provisional billed-utilization facts and left causality, access, and welfare underidentified. Round 2 discovered treatment-specific coding, preserved the historical raw claim, versioned the frozen-definition claim, and backtracked from unstable measurement. Round 3 established selection on forecast demand and provider exits with no common support, so the county causal branch was abandoned rather than repaired with matching or weighting.

A separate randomized invitation was forked for a narrow high-travel-barrier population. The state correctly retained nonreceipt, possible shared-clinician interference, null uncertainty, and a test that distinguishes net access expansion from capacity rationing. All old claim versions and branch decisions remained in the YAML artifacts.

The independent audit found one status error: the reported 15-point invitation estimate was labeled `supported` before design-consistent uncertainty and outcome auditing. It should remain `provisional` until reproduced.

Verdict: `mixed`; mainline state and backtracking criterion `proved`.

Protocol change: add explicit claim-status requirements so credible assignment does not by itself promote an unaudited point estimate.

Macro coverage now has a strict fiscal/OLG pass and multi-turn research state has direct behavioral evidence. The next upstream gaps are broader macro regimes, automatic trigger behavior, Claude Code runtime, and final legacy-skill replacement.
