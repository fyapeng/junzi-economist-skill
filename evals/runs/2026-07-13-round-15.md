# Round 15 — monetary-fiscal closure, incomplete markets, and open-economy risk

## Purpose

Test macroeconomic law through closed models rather than topic labels. Three blind endpoints covered a nonmonotone seigniorage schedule with debt, an Aiyagari production economy, and a risky two-period small open economy. Failed evidence had to remain visible, generate a general rule at the correct layer, and pass an independently worded exact-commit transfer before the macro requirement could advance.

## Initial endpoints

`JE-X038` derived the Cagan seigniorage correspondence, debt account, and welfare boundary correctly, but failed the required policy and computational closure. Its fiscal-dominant section supplied only a target steady-state identity: natural off-path completions either let debt deviations grow at rate `r` or freeze them at their initial value, and neither selected a branch or terminal condition. Its absolute tolerance also changed exact economic regions, returning one zero root for a small positive deficit and a tangency for points strictly above and below the peak. Two independent audits reproduced both defects; the stricter verdict is retained as `fail`.

`JE-X039` truly solved and independently reproduced an incomplete-markets production equilibrium. Household KKT conditions, EGM policy, lottery distribution, factor prices, assets, resources, Bellman and Euler diagnostics, tails, and sensitivities were numerically sound. The packaged verifier nevertheless read old JSON summaries instead of resolving the model, the equilibrium search returned the first coarse sign change, and the claimed upper-grid filter was not executed. Its status remains `mixed`.

`JE-X040` passed. It closed external-asset timing, trade and current accounts, all borrowing-limit KKT regimes, common-feasible-set precautionary saving, sudden stops and strict reversals, terminal assets, and welfare limits. The audit noted that budget-set nesting under Arrow trade would additionally require a no-arbitrage state-price normalization, ownership, and counterparties.

## First rule revision and transfer

Commit `d6866bb` added three transferable requirements: distinguish stationary compatibility from an executable off-steady-state policy regime; keep exact economic regions separate from residual tolerances; and require fresh recomputation, reliable full scans, all brackets, and executed contamination filters.

On that exact commit, `JE-X041` transferred the rules to the different curve `S(pi)=A*pi/(1+alpha*pi)^2`. It closed monetary- and fiscal-dominant dynamics, branch and tool domains, expectations and terminal assumptions, finite roots including the high branch escaping at zero, and 100-digit tests immediately on both sides of the peak. An independent rerun plus 48 additional property checks passed.

`JE-X042` improved the heterogeneous equilibrium materially: it freshly recomputed a new three-state economy, saved the full scan, rejected contaminated endpoints, collected all reliable brackets, and bounded the uniqueness and welfare claims. It remained `mixed` because household optimality was reported but not part of the reliability flag, its root callback did not reject an unreliable internal evaluation, its absolute-residual tangency routine rediscovered the ordinary crossing, and its joint grid change did not isolate convergence.

## Second rule revision and transfer

Commit `845a0ae` made every decisive diagnostic part of executable acceptance, required the complete root trace, distinguished a same-sign signed-residual extremum from an ordinary crossing, and required one-dimension-at-a-time numerical comparisons.

`JE-X043` passed on that exact commit. Its first asset bound was rejected under the original tail and clipping thresholds, so the solver backtracked to a wider domain without relaxing acceptance. Three fresh configurations then held all else fixed while changing only asset-grid density or only the upper bound. Every scan point, root-path evaluation, and final root was jointly gated by household optimality, distribution, market, resource, tail, clipping, and policy diagnostics; all reliable sign changes were retained; the non-crossing screen excluded crossing neighborhoods; and root and residual differences were reported. A fresh-directory audit reproduced byte-identical results and independently reproduced rejection of the abandoned small-bound branch.

## Decision

Mark `macroeconomic_law` proved for the current release-candidate audit. OLG fiscal transition, monetary-fiscal interaction, incomplete markets and finance, open-economy risk, aggregate uncertainty, distribution, dynamics, and closure now have executable adversarial evidence. Preserve the failed and mixed endpoints because they show why the final rules exist. Keep `computation_and_instruments` partial: macro equilibrium computation is now strong, while broader data pipelines, alternative algorithms, hardware variation, and additional failure regions remain unfinished.
