# Round 11 — real PDF and long-manuscript legacy comparison

## Purpose

Test gradual replacement against strong legacy baselines rather than assuming that upstream governance makes downstream skills obsolete.

## Real PDF deep reading

Both pipelines read the same 69-page April 2026 revision of *Rationing by Race*, including equations, figures, tables, appendices, references, and version header.

- `JE-X021`, paper-deep-read plus PDF only: `pass`. It accurately reconstructed the sample, pressure measure, equations, main mortality result, wait-time and text mechanisms, selection tests, and policy boundaries. Minor defects concerned an appendix physical-page range, translation of inpatient bed, and incomplete discussion of several appendix tables.
- `JE-X022`, Junzi Economist upstream plus legacy reading/PDF downstream: `mixed`. It added material economic judgment about local partial equilibrium, absolute versus relative risk, strongest rivals, failure conditions, and policy counterfactual boundaries. It also introduced a method error absent from the legacy note by calling the Race by HighStrain two-way interaction a triple difference, and slightly simplified one text-index component.

Conclusion: integration adds real judgment but is not monotonically more accurate. Paper-reading guidance now requires exact interaction order, transformations, and inference labels.

## Long-manuscript writing

Both pipelines received the same fixed telemedicine evidence packet and produced a compliant abstract and 1,500–2,000 word introduction without outside citations.

- `JE-X023`, econ-paper only: `pass`. Every number and boundary for ITT, receipt, travel, nulls, observational spillovers, exploratory subgroup, interference, external validity, and welfare was preserved.
- `JE-X024`, Junzi Economist upstream plus econ-paper downstream: `pass`. It was somewhat tighter and added an inspectable claim audit, but the manuscript was only marginally safer than the already strong legacy output.

Conclusion: upstream governance has material process value and ensures inspectability; it has not yet demonstrated a large standalone prose advantage.

## Mainline decision

Do not delete `econ-paper` or `paper-deep-read`. Retain a documented coexistence architecture: Junzi Economist governs economic objects, claim status, branches, and welfare upstream; legacy skills remain optional downstream execution capabilities. Reopen retirement only after multiple theory, structural, empirical, bilingual, and public-explanation comparisons show no accuracy loss and all remaining unique tools are reproduced or unnecessary.
