# Round 13 — agency, power, and genuine Dao conflict

## Purpose

Test whether Junzi is an operative economic discipline rather than moral language appended after an efficiency calculation. The endpoint had to make constrained agency, asymmetric power, safety, due process, collective voice, rights constraints, and unresolved public values change the economic object, feasible set, evidence standard, welfare criterion, experiment, and branch decision.

## Iterative forward tests

Five isolated tasks moved across platform labor, mandatory AI proctoring, public housing, specialist appointments, and need-based scholarships. Each response was reviewed by two independent auditors. Initial defects were preserved instead of erased, then converted into concise general rules and retested:

- hypothetical survey responses are stated preferences, not true or revealed preferences;
- dependence and limited outside options are distinct from another actor's penalty, opacity, retaliation, or control;
- payments, real resources, producer surplus, accounting profit, normal returns, rents, taxes, ownership, and shifted costs require one consistent social account;
- missing no-loser evidence leaves Pareto improvement unsupported rather than contradicted, and Kaldor–Hicks potential compensation differs from compensation paid;
- every experimental arm must share a nonrandomizable safety, access, privacy, due-process, nonretaliation, and exit floor;
- protected choice changes the assignment and estimand: policy offer ITT is not selected receipt;
- fixed capacity makes displacement and interference constitutive, requiring capacity pools, exposure mappings, saturation-specific ITTs, spillovers, total-policy effects, and bounded transport;
- randomized assignment, peer exposure, and endogenous receipt require separate notation and potential outcomes;
- an exclusion threat must remain live under the tested protection floor; a floor breach is protocol failure, not useful variation;
- exact fixed-count assignment changes realized peer exposure by one unit, so a same-exposure direct effect needs common support or must be relabeled as the design-supported compound contrast.

## Endpoint judgment

The final scholarship transfer independently applied the revised skill across all central Dao and economic requirements. One red-team auditor found a finite-population support error that the other rated nonblocking; the response and skill returned to the design layer, corrected the estimand, and both auditors passed the retest at full marks. This history demonstrates the intended behavior: no defense of a plausible-looking branch, no erasure of failed states, and no use of values to excuse technical imprecision.

`junzi_root` is now proved for the release-candidate audit. The evidence spans seven institutional domains and shows that human concern changes economic analysis and action. Remaining expansion is breadth, not absence of a dedicated behavior endpoint.

Two final committed-state checks strengthened that judgment. `JE-X032` on commit `2e2c603` revealed that adjacent counts do not create common exposure across heterogeneous pool sizes and that outcome bounds are not effect bounds. Those failures were converted into exact-N/exposure-targeted support and `[L-U,U-L]` transport rules. `JE-X033` then ran blind on exact commit `b8f4611` in a new childcare-allocation setting. Both independent auditors passed it without revision, one at 105/105. The clean committed run, not the corrected development outputs alone, is the release evidence.
